About TasteHub

Fresh food, fast delivery, zero hassle.

Our Story

TasteHub started in 2024 because we were tired of cold, late, and overpriced food delivery. We built a platform that connects you directly to kitchens that cook fresh, pack fast, and deliver hot. Now we serve Nairobi with burgers, pizza, snacks, and more.

What We Do

🍔 Fresh Meals

Food is cooked after you order. No pre-made, no warmers. Just fresh.

⚡ Fast Delivery

25-45 min delivery across Nairobi. Live tracking and WhatsApp updates.

💳 Easy Payment

Pay with M-Pesa, card, or cash on delivery. No hidden fees.

Our Standards

  • Quality Check: Every order is checked before it leaves the kitchen.
  • Real Support: Reach a human on WhatsApp in under 2 minutes.
  • Fair Pricing: You see the final price at checkout. No surprises.
  • Make it Right: Wrong order or cold food? We re-make it or refund you.
