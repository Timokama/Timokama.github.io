Returns & Refunds Policy

We want you happy with every order. Here’s how returns work for food orders.

1. Food Quality Issues

If your food arrives cold, wrong, damaged, or not as described, contact us within 30 minutes of delivery on WhatsApp.

  • We’ll re-make and re-deliver at no extra cost, or
  • Give you a full refund to M-Pesa or your original payment method

2. Wrong Order

Got the wrong item? Send us a photo on WhatsApp within 15 minutes of delivery. We’ll fix it immediately or refund the item.

3. Order Cancellations

You can cancel for free within 5 minutes of placing the order. After that, we may not be able to cancel if food prep has started.

4. What We Can’t Refund

  • Orders eaten or partially consumed
  • Returns requested more than 1 hour after delivery
  • Change of mind on taste preference after food is delivered

5. How to Request a Return/Refund

  1. Take a photo of the issue
  2. Message us on WhatsApp: +254 768 394 866
  3. Include your order number and issue description
  4. We’ll respond within 1 hour and resolve it same day
