Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?

Orders in Nairobi are delivered within 24-48 hours. Other counties take 2-5 business days depending on location.

Do you offer cash on delivery?

Yes, cash on delivery is available in Nairobi and select towns. Pay on delivery with cash or M-Pesa.

Are products original?

100% genuine. All electronics come with manufacturer warranty. We source directly from authorized distributors.

How do I track my order?

Once shipped, you’ll receive a WhatsApp message with tracking details. You can also contact us on 0768 394 866.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled before they’re shipped. Contact us immediately on WhatsApp to cancel.

Do you have a physical store?

We’re currently online only. Visit us at shopmart.co.ke or contact us on WhatsApp for support.
