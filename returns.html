Returns & Refunds Policy

We want you to shop with confidence

7-Day Return Policy

You can return most items within 7 days of delivery if:

  • Item is unused, in original packaging
  • No physical damage or tampering
  • Defective or wrong item delivered
Note: Personal care items and opened electronics cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How to Request a Return

  • Contact us on WhatsApp at 0768 394 866 within 7 days
  • Share order number and reason for return
  • Our team will arrange pickup or guide you to drop-off point
  • Refund processed within 3-5 business days after inspection

Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to your original payment method:

  • M-Pesa: Sent directly to your phone
  • Bank Transfer: 2-3 business days
  • Store Credit: Instant, valid for 6 months

Warranty Claims

Electronics come with 6-12 months manufacturer warranty. For warranty issues:

  • Contact us with proof of purchase
  • We’ll coordinate with the service center
  • Repair, replacement, or refund based on warranty terms
