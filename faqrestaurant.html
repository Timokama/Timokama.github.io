Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery takes 25-45 minutes depending on your location. You’ll get a WhatsApp update when your order is on the way.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver within Nairobi CBD and a 10km radius. Check at checkout to confirm if we reach you.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel within 5 minutes of placing the order. Message us on WhatsApp immediately.

Do you accept M-Pesa?

Yes! Pay on delivery via M-Pesa or cash. Lipa na M-Pesa details will be sent to you.

What if my order is wrong?

Contact us on WhatsApp within 30 minutes of delivery and we’ll fix it or refund you.
